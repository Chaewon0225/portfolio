AISOI

Yusong created this poster for the UROP Symposium held in April, 2025. Click here to try our game!

Collaborators: Alex Barrett

Mentors: Collin May (2023-2024),Kayla Cardenas (2023-2025),Yusong Liang (2024-2026)Nicky Ven (2025-2026)Laila McCullers (2025-2026)

I have been working with Sun Young Park, a Ph.D. Candidate in Communications and my ex-roommate, to create a serious game on promoting mindful eating and intuitive eating principles. We have been recruiting and working with 5 undergraduate research assistants since 2023. The game is currently available at https://yusongl.itch.io/nutriquest-final We're working on improving the user interface and augmenting the game content. We plan our third iteration of user testing in Spring 2026. All the visual assets used in the game were created by Kayla Cardenas and Nicky Ven, our two amazing undergraduate research assistants. Yusong Liang, another awesome undergraduate research assistant, developed the game in Unity and has an open repository on GitHub. I was nominated for the 2024 UROP Best Mentor Award for leading this project. This project has been internally funded by the FSU Center for Undergraduate Research and Academic Engagement since 2023 (total $1,500).

We have finished two rounds of user testing. We're working on qualitative analysis of the user testing to inform our third iteration and our design-based research paper. The third iteration of user testing is expected in Spring 2026. We are having conversations with the nutrition center at FSU for potential collaboration and participant recruitment.
