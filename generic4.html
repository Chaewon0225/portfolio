IBSTPI

We won the 3rd place at the IBSTPI Instructor Resources Competition in 2024. View the announcement here.

Collaborators: Alex Barrett, Jaesung Hur, Veronica Brewer

Mentor: Kadir Kozan, Ph.D.

I led a team of doctoral colleagues in instructional design for the IBSTPI project. For this competition, we designed resources for instructional designers to familiarize with IBSTPI competencies. The final product can be viewed here.

We won about $400 from the competition, which we spent together for a nice dinner at a local restaurant. The food was so good that the only photo we remembered to take was of the desserts.
