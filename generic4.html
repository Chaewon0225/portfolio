IBSTPI

We won the 3rd place at the IBSTPI Instructor Resources Competition in 2024. View the announcement here.

Collaborators: Alex Barrett, Jaesung Hur, Veronica Brewer

Mentor: Kadir Kozan, Ph.D.

We have finished two rounds of user testing. We're working on qualitative analysis of the user testing to inform our third iteration and our design-based research paper. The third iteration of user testing is expected in Spring 2026. We are having conversations with the nutrition center at FSU for potential collaboration and participant recruitment.
